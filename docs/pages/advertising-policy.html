Advertising Policy

Foretoken maintains a strict separation between our editorial and advertising teams. Sponsored content is always clearly labeled, and we never allow advertisers to influence our reporting or rankings.

We accept advertising partnerships that align with our mission and provide real value to our audience. We reserve the right to reject any advertiser or sponsored material that we find misleading, unethical, or irrelevant to our community.
