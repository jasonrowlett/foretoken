ABOUT FORETOKEN

We are the future of tokenized asset intelligence.

Foretoken was founded on a simple conviction: that transparency is the missing bridge between traditional finance and tokenized markets. We're building the Bloomberg Terminal for tokenized real-world assets — a platform that unifies physical and digital asset data for investors, researchers, and institutions.

As crypto matures and regulatory clarity improves, the digitization of commodities, real estate, treasuries, and private credit will become a pillar of financial infrastructure. Our mission is to empower that transformation by delivering clarity, insight, and market data through a trusted lens.

Whether you're a hedge fund, researcher, allocator, or builder — Foretoken equips you with the context and intelligence needed to lead in the RWA era.

About Me

Jason Rowlett is the founder of Foretoken, a media and data intelligence platform focused on Real-World Asset (RWA) tokenization. He is the creator and trademark holder of the Double Pedigree Investing Strategy, a comparative framework designed to evaluate tokenized assets against their traditional counterparts.

A journalist and speaker with a background in global finance, history, and tokenomics, Jason brings clarity to complex market shifts at the intersection of blockchain and traditional investing. He has spoken at leading Web3 events across Europe and is dedicated to helping institutions understand and adopt tokenized financial instruments.
