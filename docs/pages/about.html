About Foretoken

Foretoken is a media and analytics platform that helps investors navigate the world of tokenized real-world assets (RWA). We bridge the gap between traditional finance and on-chain innovation by comparing RWA tokens like XAUT and PAXG with their physical counterparts, such as gold and treasuries.

Our mission is to educate, equip, and empower investors through:

  • Side-by-side data comparisons
  • Market insights and news
  • Original reporting and interviews
  • Interactive dashboards and eBooks

We are committed to transparency, clarity, and making tokenization understandable for everyone.
